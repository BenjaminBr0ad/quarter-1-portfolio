About Me

After graduating from University of Colorado Boulder in 2014 with a B.A. of Psychology, I spent four years managing medical cannabis dispensaries in Denver. My passion for helping others led me to the cannabis industry, and I enjoyed leading a team of like-minded individuals and connecting with patients from all walks of life. My drive to grow and develop as an individual and my need for challenge has led me into the world of web devlopment, where I create elegant and attractive user interfaces that shape the user experience of innovative and intelligent products. I am proficient in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript, and look forward to ever expanding my toolkit.

The Zen Buddhist ensō (円相 , "circle") has been a guiding force in my journey. Drawn daily in a single brush-stroke as an act of meditation, it represents a moment in which the mind was free to let the body create something perfect, and yet inherently flawed. A snapshot of this particular moment in time, each ensō represents strength, elegance and simplicity. They are often left open to represent infinite room for growth and improvement, while still being beautiful as they are. To me, the ensō represents the balance between change and inner-peace, and serves as a reminder of impermance.
